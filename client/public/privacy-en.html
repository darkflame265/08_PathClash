PathClash Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

PathClash ("the Service") processes the minimum personal information required to provide gameplay, account services, in-app purchases, and stable service operations. This Privacy Policy applies to both the web and app versions of the Service.

1. Information We Collect

The Service may collect and store the following information:

1) Account Information

  • Supabase anonymous account ID
  • Google account identifier (Google User ID) when using Google Sign-In
  • Legal consent version and consent timestamp

2) Profile and Progress Information

  • Nickname
  • Equipped skin and owned skins
  • Achievement progress, completion, and claim history

3) Gameplay Information

  • Win/loss records
  • Match records and gameplay-related data
  • Virtual token balance

4) Payment Information

  • Google Play purchase token
  • Purchased product ID and pack ID
  • Granted token amount and purchase record

Payment card information is processed by the applicable payment platform, such as Google Play, and is not directly stored by the Service.

5) Account Linking Information

  • Guest-to-account merge history used to preserve records and progress across account upgrades

6) Technical Information

The following may be collected automatically:

  • IP address
  • Access logs
  • Request logs
  • Device and browser information

This information is used for service security and operational stability.

2. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Login, guest account handling, and Google account linking
  • Multiplayer matchmaking, AI matches, and gameplay processing
  • Saving records, tokens, skins, achievements, and account-linked progress
  • Verifying in-app purchases and preventing duplicate reward grants
  • Maintaining account merge history when upgrading from guest to linked accounts
  • Recording legal consent status
  • Service security, abuse prevention, and stable operations
  • Error analysis and service improvement

3. Retention and Deletion

Information is retained according to the following criteria:

  • Account, profile, gameplay, token, skin, achievement, and consent data: until account deletion is requested or service operation ends
  • Purchase verification records: retained as needed to prevent duplicate payment rewards, resolve support issues, and meet operational requirements
  • Access and technical logs: up to 30 days, then deleted

If a user requests account deletion, related personal information will be deleted within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by applicable law or necessary for fraud prevention and payment verification.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: user authentication and database processing
  • Google OAuth: Google account sign-in integration
  • Google Play Billing: in-app purchase processing and verification

Each service follows its own privacy policy.

5. User Rights

Users may request:

  • Access to personal information
  • Correction of personal information
  • Deletion of personal information

Requests can be submitted through the contact below.

6. Data Deletion Request

Users may request account and personal data deletion by email. After verification, related personal information will be deleted within a reasonable period.
